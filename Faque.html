1. Ese mubyukuri nppa niki? nizihe service itanga??

NPPA: natinal public procecution Authority

Nppa ni ikigo kigihugu gishizwe ubushinza cyaha bukuru ...................

Servise dutanga

  • Icyangobwa cyuko utakatiwe ninkiko
  • Gukurikirana dosiye yo kureba ibyaha bimunga ubukungu
  • Gukurikirana dosiye zibyaha byihohorerwa N'ibiyobyabwenge
  • Kurinda abahohotewe nabatanga buhamya

    • Niba ushaka kureba ibisobanuro birambuye wakanda aha NPPA
