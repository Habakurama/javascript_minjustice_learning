Sex Offender Registry (SOR)

Ese Sex Offender Registry Niki?

Ni Urutonde rw'abafashweho ibyaha by’ihohotera rishingiye ku gitsina (SOR) ni uburyo bukoreshwa na za guverinoma mu bihugu byinshi bwo gukurikirana no gukurikiranira hafi abantu bahanwe kubera ibyaha runaka by'ihohotera rishingiye ku gitsina. Bugamije kongera umutekano rusange butanga amakuru ku bantu bashinjwe ibyo byaha by’ihohotera ku nzego z’umutekano, kandi mu bihe byinshi, ayo makuru agashyirwa no mu baturage.

Intego ya Sex Offender Registry

  • Kongera ubumenyi ku mutekano rusange"
  • Gukumira gusubira gukora ibyaha
  • Gufasha inzego z’umutekano mu iperereza
  • Gutanga ibikoresho byo gusesengura no gucunga ibyago
  • Korohereza itangazo ry'abaturage igihe bikenewe

Nigute Registry ikora

Iyo umuntu ahamwe n'icyaha cy'ihohotera rishingiye ku gitsina riri mu byemewe, asabwa kwiyandikisha ku nzego z'umutekano z'aho atuye. Kwiyandikisha bisanzwe birimo:

  • Amakuru yihariye (amazina, aderesi, ifoto)
  • Amakuru ajyanye N’icyaha
  • Ibisabwa mu kubika amakuru n'igihe bigomba gukorwa

Abanditswe ku rutonde basabwa gukomeza kuvugurura amakuru yabo, kandi bashobora guhura n’ingaruka z’amategeko niba batubahirije amabwiriza

I kitonderwa kingenzi

Nubwo Urutonde rw’abahamijwe ibyaha by’ihohotera rishingiye ku gitsina ari igikoresho cy'ingenzi mu kurinda umutekano rusange, ni ngombwa gukoresha aya makuru mu buryo buboneye. Gukorera ihohotera cyangwa gukoresha imbaraga mu buryo bw’ubwicanyi ku bantu banditswe ku rutonde nti byemewe n’amategeko kandi bigabanya uburyo bwo gukumira ibyaha.

Kugera ku Makuru y’Urutonde

"Mu bihugu byinshi, amakuru y’urutonde rw’abafashweho ibyaha by’ihohotera rishingiye ku gitsina aboneka ku mbuga za interineti ku baturage. Ariko, uburyo amakuru aboneka ku mugaragaro butandukanye bitewe n’aho umuntu aherereye, kandi bukurikiza amategeko n’amabwiriza agenga ibanga ry’amakuru."
